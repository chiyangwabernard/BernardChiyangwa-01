Musa Family Catering specializes in peri peri chicken and other related products. We use only fresh, natural ingredients to make our unique sauce and grill our chicken over a charcoal flame to give it an extra special flavour. For those who cant get enough of chilies, we offer a hot version, and for those who prefer their food without it, our "mild" sauce has all the flavour without the heat.
